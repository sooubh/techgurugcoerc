AI-Powered Parenting & Therapy Companion for Children with Disabilities
1. Product Overview

CARE-AI is a mobile application designed to support parents and caregivers of children with developmental or physical disabilities through AI-driven personalized guidance, structured therapy modules, interactive activities, and expert supervision.

Unlike generic chatbots, CARE-AI combines:

Expert-designed therapy lessons stored in a database

AI-based personalization and recommendation

Interactive child activities and games

Progress tracking and adaptive learning

Doctor/therapist oversight portal

The system enables affordable, home-based intervention while keeping professionals in control.

Important: CARE-AI is NOT a diagnostic tool and does not replace medical advice.
